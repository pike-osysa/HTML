Kenya is a vibrant country located in East Africa, known for its diverse landscapes, rich wildlife, and cultural heritage. From the bustling capital city of Nairobi to the stunning savannahs of the Maasai Mara, Kenya offers a unique blend of modern urban life and natural beauty. It is renowned for its role in wildlife conservation and is a popular destination for safaris. With over 40 ethnic groups, Kenya boasts a rich cultural tapestry and a dynamic society that continues to grow and evolve.

INTRODUCTION

Autism is a developmental condition that affects how a person communicates, interacts, and experiences the world. It varies widely from person to person and is often referred to as autism spectrum disorder (ASD). People with autism may have unique strengths and face challenges in social situations or sensory processing.
